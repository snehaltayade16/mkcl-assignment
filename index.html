Incredible Learning Innovations for Knowledge Empowerment

MKCL offers career-oriented courses that help students enter emerging local and global careers by aligning with industry roles, technologies, and certification standards. These courses foster job readiness, confidence, and a growth mindset. Under the ‘iLike’ brand, MKCL provides creditized courses as per the National Credit Framework (NCrF) 2023, bridging college to careers.

Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited (MKCL)

Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited (MKCL) was established by the Department of Higher & Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra under the Companies Act, 1956. Since January 5, 2018, its coordination has been managed by the General Administration Department (GAD) and later assigned to the Directorate of Information Technology under GAD.
